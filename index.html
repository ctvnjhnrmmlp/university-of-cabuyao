History

Established in 2003 through Municipal Ordinance No. 2003-059, the Pamantasan ng Cabuyao (PnC) has evolved into a beacon of educational excellence. Inaugurated by President Gloria Macapagal Arroyo, PnC officially commenced operations on July 31, 2003, dedicated to providing quality education. Recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and granted the privilege of FREE COLLEGE EDUCATION under Republic Act 10931, PnC stands as one of the select few local colleges and universities in the Philippines with this distinction.

With accreditation from ALCUCOA for twelve Level 2 and two Level 1 programs, PnC emphasizes free quality education, producing topnotchers in education, nursing, and engineering. Looking ahead, the university aspires to be the premier institution in Region IV, raising academic standards and molding globally competent, value-laden graduates. PnC's commitment to holistic education aims to cultivate 'Dangal ng Bayan,' individuals who bring pride and honor to the nation. Join us on our journey of academic excellence and nation-building.

Mission

As an institution of higher learning, UC (PnC) is committed to equip individuals with knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them to achieve their professional goals & provide leadership and service for national development.

Vision

A premier institution of higher learning in Region IV, developing globally-competitive and value-laden professionals and leaders instrumental to community development and nation-building

Core Values

As a God-fearing institution respecting multi-faith of people, PnC adheres to the following core values: Personal Dignity, Nurturing Community, and Commitment to Excellence
